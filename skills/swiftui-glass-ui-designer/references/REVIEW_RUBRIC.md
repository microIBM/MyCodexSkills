# Review Rubric

Use this rubric to judge whether a glass redesign pass is ready to hand back.

Score each item from 1 to 5. A score below 4 needs either a fix or an explicit limitation in the final response.

## Visual coherence

The app uses one consistent visual language across screens. Materials, radii, spacing, shadows, strokes, and selected states feel intentional rather than accumulated.

## Native macOS feel

The app feels like a SwiftUI macOS app, not a web app or generic template. Controls remain recognizable and platform-appropriate.

## Reusability

Glass styling is implemented through reusable components, modifiers, styles, or tokens. Repeated one-off materials, radii, strokes, shadows, and animations have been consolidated.

## Readability

Text and controls remain clear in normal use. Glass depth supports hierarchy without weakening contrast.

## Accessibility

Reduce Transparency, light mode, dark mode, keyboard focus, and selected/hover states are handled intentionally.

## Restraint

The glass effect is subtle and premium rather than blurry, noisy, decorative, or over-animated.

## Product preservation

The product's behavior, data flow, routing, calculations, actions, and main user flows are preserved. No business logic was changed.

## Build health

The app builds successfully, or the final response reports the exact environmental blocker. The final response includes the command used.
